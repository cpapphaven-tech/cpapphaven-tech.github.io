✍️ About ABC Tracing

Play ABC Tracing, the perfect educational game for preschoolers to practice handwriting! Watch your toddler learn and evolve with this fun, intuitive, and highly interactive web app. Master writing the entire English alphabet (both uppercase ABC and lowercase abc), numbers 1-10, and begin tracing full words categorized into animals, fruits, and family.

🎮 How to Play

  • ✅ Select Category: Choose from Uppercase Letters, Lowercase Letters, Numbers, or Words.
  • ✅ Trace: Follow the dashed outline using your finger (on mobile) or mouse to draw the shape.
  • ✅ Learn & Associate: Each letter comes with a fun word association to build vocabulary (e.g., "A is for Apple 🍎").
  • ✅ Complete Words: Challenge yourself by tracing full words like "CAT", "BANANA", or "MOM"!
