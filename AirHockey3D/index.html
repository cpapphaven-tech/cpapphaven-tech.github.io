🏑 About Air Hockey 3D

Air Hockey 3D brings the classic arcade table game to your screen in a stunning neon-lit 3D environment. Challenge an intelligent AI opponent in fast-paced matches that will test your reflexes and strategic thinking. Experience smooth gliding physics, intense gameplay, and satisfying sound effects as you compete to score goals and become the undisputed champion of the rink.

🎮 How to Play

  • ✅ Drag your mallet to defend your goal and hit the puck
  • ✅ Try to bounce the puck off walls to trick the AI
  • ✅ Score points by getting the puck into the opponent's goal
  • ✅ The first player to score 7 points wins the match
  • ✅ Use swift movements for stronger strikes
