🕳️ About All in Hole

All in Hole is the oddly satisfying black-hole puzzle where you guide Molly the mole on a snack-swallowing adventure. Slide a tiny hole, devour small objects to grow, and use playful physics to gobble every target treat. Whether you're here to zen out or chase trophies, All in Hole serves up quick, colorful levels that feel great to master.

🎮 How to Play

  • ✅ Drag to move the black hole across the play area
  • ✅ Only eat the golden glowing target treats — avoid everything else!
  • ✅ Eating a wrong item ends your run immediately
  • ✅ Swallow enough targets to complete each level
  • ✅ Plan your route carefully to avoid danger

💡 Tips from Molly

Look ahead: plan a clean route so you don't touch the wrong items.
Patience: wait for the right targets to come close to your hole.
Prioritize: gulp the light targets first, then the big ones.
