About Archery Master

Play free online Archery Master: Math Balloon Shooter – an addictive fusion of precision archery and fast-paced math! Step onto the arcade range, pull back your bow, and aim at rising balloons. Instead of traditional targets, you must pop specific numbered balloons to rack up points before the 60-second timer hits zero! With unlimited progressive levels, each stage brings a higher target score constraint. Be careful! Popping red negative balloons will actively deduct from your score and hinder your progress. Whether you are an archery fan or a math wizard, this unique hybrid game delivers satisfying slingshot mechanics designed perfectly for both mobile and desktop.

  • 🏹 Realistic pull-and-drag slingshot bow physics
  • 🎈 Math-based floating balloon targets
  • ⏱️ Fast-paced 60-second arcade timer levels
  • 📉 Avoid negative balloons to maintain a high score
  • 📱 Full mobile touch support – drag to predict your arrow arc in portrait mode!
  • 🎮 Free and unblocked with no downloads required

How to Play Archery Master

  • Aim: Tap and hold the slingshot at the bottom of the screen, and pull backward.
  • Predict: Use the dotted arc trajectory line to predict where your arrow will land.
  • Shoot: Release your finger (or mouse) to fire the arrow into the sky!
  • Score: Balloons have random numeric values (e.g. +15, +30). Pop them to increase your level score.
  • Beware: Red numbered balloons have negative values (e.g. -12). Shooting these will drain your hard-earned points!
  • Advance: Reach the goal score before the 60 seconds run out to instantly clear the level.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Archery Master free to play?

Yes! Archery Master is 100% free with no sign-up or download required. Play directly in your browser on any device.

Does Archery Master work on mobile?

Absolutely. The game is specifically designed for mobile devices. Simply hold your phone in Portrait mode, tap, pull back, and release to shoot your arrow.

How many levels are there?

Archery Master features unlimited continuous levels. Each time you beat a level, the required target score to pass the next round increases!

What is the best strategy to win?

Focus on timing your shots to hit the largest positive balloons while avoiding clusters of red negative balloons! Don't spam your arrows—one poorly timed shot cutting through a string of minus balloons can ruin your level.

Does my score get saved?

Yes! Your all-time best score and highest level reached are automatically saved locally in your browser's storage.
