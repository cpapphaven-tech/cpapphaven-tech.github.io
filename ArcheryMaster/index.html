How to Play Archery Master

  • Aim: Your bow bobs up and down automatically — time your shot perfectly.
  • Shoot: Tap / click (or press Space/Enter) when the sight aligns with the center ring.
  • Score: Bullseye = 10 pts · Inner ring = 7 pts · Mid ring = 5 pts · Outer = 3 pts.
  • Bonus: 3 consecutive bullseyes trigger a 🔥 Hot Streak worth 2× score.
  • Wind: From Level 5 onward, a crosswind deflects your arrows — adjust timing!
  • Advance: Hit the required score threshold to unlock the next level.
  • Lives: You get 3 arrows per round. Missing the board entirely costs a life. Losing all lives ends the game.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Archery Master free to play?

Yes! Archery Master is 100% free with no sign-up or download required. Play directly in your browser on any device.

Does Archery Master work on mobile?

Absolutely. The game uses responsive touch controls optimised for smartphones and tablets. Just tap the screen to shoot!

How many levels are there?

Archery Master features unlimited levels. Each 10-level set introduces new mechanics such as wind, moving targets, and spinning rings, keeping the challenge fresh forever.

What is the best strategy to score high?

Watch the target's rhythm, anticipate the wind indicator, and chain bullseyes for the Hot Streak multiplier. Consistency beats lucky shots every time.

Does my score get saved?

Your best score is saved locally in your browser's storage so you can track your personal best even after closing the tab.
