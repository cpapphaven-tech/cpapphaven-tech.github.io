How to Play

Play free online Block Puzzle Dash game. This addictive brain-teaser challenges you to fit various shapes into a grid, clearing lines to score big. Experience smooth 2D graphics and increasingly difficult levels as you strive to beat your high score!

  • Drag Shapes: Drag the blocks from the bottom row onto the grid.
  • Clear Lines: Fill an entire horizontal or vertical line to clear it and score points.
  • Beat the Clock: Reach the level target before the 60-second timer runs out.
  • Strategy: Plan ahead to clear multiple lines simultaneously for massive combo bonuses!
