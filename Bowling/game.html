Master Your Throw

3D Bowling Clash is an immersive 3D bowling game where you can master the perfect curve ball. Experience realistic physics, vibrant 3D graphics, and intuitive swipe controls to beat the AI in a complete 10-frame game.

How to Play

Drag left or right before swiping up to position your ball. Then, swipe UP fast to throw it down the lane! The height of your swipe controls your power, and the left/right angle of your swipe curves the ball!

FAQ

Is this game completely free? Yes! You can play all 10 frames against the AI immediately directly in your browser.
