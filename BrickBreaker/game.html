About Brick Breaker 3D

A blazing modern reinvention of the arcade classic — rendered in real-time 3D with HDR bloom lighting, neon glowing bricks, and dynamic physics. Every hit triggers particle explosions and a dynamic light that tracks the ball across the scene.

Unlimited levels! Progress further as bricks get tougher, the grid widens, and the ball gets faster. How high can you score?

How to Play

  • Move Paddle: Move your mouse or drag on mobile.
  • Launch: Tap or click to launch the ball.
  • Break Bricks: Clear all bricks to advance a level.
  • Lives: You have 3 lives — don't let the ball fall!
