Candy Blast – Free Online Puzzle Game

Play free online Candy Blast game. Swap adjacent candies to form vertical or horizontal lines of three or more matching candies. Every match boosts your score and drops new candies from above! Challenge yourself to reach higher levels and face bigger targets.

How to Play

Tap and drag a candy towards an adjacent candy to swap them. If the swap creates a match of 3 or more identical candies, they will pop, and you'll earn points. If the swap doesn't result in a match, the candies will return to their original positions.
