How to Play

Classic chess rules with a modern neon twist:

  • Select: Tap or click a piece to select it. Valid moves will highlight in green.
  • Move: Tap a highlighted square to move your piece there.
  • Special Moves: Castling, en passant, and pawn promotion are all fully supported!
  • Check: Your king glows red when in check. You must resolve it immediately.
  • Win: Checkmate the opponent's king to win the game!
  • AI Mode: The AI thinks 2 moves ahead using minimax + alpha-beta pruning.

About Chess Master

Play free online Chess Master — a beautifully redesigned chess experience built for PlayMix. Features a neon dark-themed board, piece capture tracking, move history, full chess rules, and a smart AI opponent.

Works perfectly on mobile with large tap targets and a responsive board layout.
