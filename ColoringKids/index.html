🎨 About Coloring for Kids

A magical coloring book for toddlers aged 2–6! Color adorable animals, mighty dinosaurs, speedy cars, and ocean creatures on clean white templates, or doodle freely on a blank canvas. Completely safe and ad-free during play!

🎮 2 Creative Modes

  • 🖌️ Color: Fill picture sections with your chosen colors on white templates with black outlines.
  • 🌈 Free Draw: Paint and doodle anything you can imagine!

🌟 5 Themes

  • 🐾 Animals – Cat, Dog, Lion, Elephant & more
  • 🦕 Dinosaurs – T-Rex, Stegosaurus, Triceratops & more
  • 🚗 Cars – Race car, Truck, Rocket & more
  • 🌊 Ocean – Fish, Dolphin, Whale, Octopus & more
  • 🦄 Fantasy – Unicorn, Dragon, Castle & more
