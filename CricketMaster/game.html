About Cricket Master

Step onto the pitch and face elite bowlers in Cricket Master! This fast-paced batting simulator challenges your timing and shot selection. Can you hit the target and lead your team to victory in the World Cup finale?

How to Play

  • Observation: Watch the bowler closely as they release the ball.
  • Timing: Click or tap when the ball is in your hitting zone.
  • Placement: Different shot timings will direct the ball to different parts of the field.
  • Scoring: Aim for the high-run zones to chase down the target quickly.
