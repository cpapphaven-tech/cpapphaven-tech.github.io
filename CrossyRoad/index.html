How to Play

Navigate the sunny roads and collect number balloons:

  • Controls: Swipe to move, tap the on-screen buttons, or use Arrow keys.
  • Score: Step on a floating balloon to collect it. Watch out—some are negative!
  • Goal: Reach the Target Score before the time runs out.
  • Danger: Avoid the cars and trucks. One hit and it's Game Over!

About Cross Road with Balloon

Play free online Cross Road with Balloon — a beautifully redesigned 3D arcade experience built for PlayMix. Features a vibrant daylight-themed road, collectible number balloons, real-time proximity detection, and a smart scaling score system.

Works perfectly on mobile with large tap targets and a responsive board layout.
