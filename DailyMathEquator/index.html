How to Play Daily Math Equator

  • Guess the Equation: Type an 8-character math equation using the on-screen keypad or your physical keyboard. Example: 12+05=17
  • Submit a Guess: Press the ENTER key or tap the ✓ button after typing a complete valid equation.
  • 🟩 Green Tile: The digit or symbol is in the CORRECT position in the secret equation.
  • 🟨 Yellow Tile: The digit or symbol IS in the equation, but in a DIFFERENT position.
  • ⬛ Gray Tile: The digit or symbol is NOT present in the secret equation at all.
  • Win: Guess the exact equation within 6 attempts to score a win! Share your result and come back tomorrow!

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Daily Math Equator free to play?

Yes! 100% free with no sign-up or app download required. Open your browser and play today's equation puzzle instantly.

How is Daily Math Equator different from Wordle?

Instead of guessing a word, you guess a hidden math equation! Both use the same green/yellow/gray color-coded feedback system, but Daily Math Equator challenges your arithmetic reasoning and logical deduction skills.

Does everyone get the same equation every day?

Yes – the equation is seeded by today's calendar date, so everybody around the world is solving the same daily puzzle. Compare your results with family and friends!

What kinds of equations can appear?

Equations use addition (+), subtraction (−), and multiplication (×) with results that always produce a valid arithmetic expression. All equations have exactly 8 characters including the equals sign.

Does playing Daily Math Equator improve math skills?

Absolutely! Consistent practice with number patterns, mental arithmetic, and equation logical deduction strengthens your brain's numerical processing – especially effective for students and adults seeking daily cognitive training.
