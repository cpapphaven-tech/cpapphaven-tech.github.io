About Smiley Doodle Jump

Play free online Smiley Doodle Jump game. Bounce upwards across a continuously generating array of synthwave platforms. Master the brand-new double jump mechanics to traverse massive gaps and rack up high scores!

Game Rules

  • Objective: Survive as long as possible by jumping up platforms. Falling below the bottom of the screen ends the game.
  • Controls: Use the Left and Right arrow keys, or A and D, or tap the mobile control buttons to move horizontally. You wrap around the screen edges!
  • Platforms: Green platforms are solid. Brown platforms will break upon touch. Blue platforms move horizontally.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a high score?

Focus on landing on green platforms and try to hit spring boxes to leap past difficult sections quickly without risking a fall.

Is the game mobile-friendly?

Yes! Intuitive mobile touch buttons are overlaid at the bottom of the screen rendering it completely playable on phones and tablets.
