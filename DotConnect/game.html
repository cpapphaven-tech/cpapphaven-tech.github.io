About Dot Connect

Play free online Dot Connect game. Connect lines of Dots, relax with fun puzzles, and test your brain in this captivating game about making connections! Connect dots of the same color vertically or horizontally to eliminate them and score points.

Find your flow and connect the colors across unlimited brain-teasing adventures. Every level brings a higher target score, but you only have 60 seconds!

How to Play

  • Connect: Touch & drag through adjacent same-colored dots (up, down, left, right).
  • Match: Connect at least 2 dots to eliminate them.
  • Make a Square: Connect dots into a closed loop to eliminate ALL dots of that color from the board!
  • Level Up: Hit the target score before 60 seconds run out.
