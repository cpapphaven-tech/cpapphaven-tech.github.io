How to Play English Grammar Quiz

  • Choose Your Answer: Read the question and tap one of the four answer options (A, B, C, D).
  • Timer: You have 30 seconds per question. Answer quickly to keep your score high!
  • Green = Correct: You gain points and advance to the next question.
  • Red = Wrong: The game ends — but you keep your score and can try again!
  • Level Up: Every 10 correct answers advances you to the next level with harder grammar topics and more points per question.
  • Use Lifelines: Stuck? Use ✂️ 50:50 to remove 2 wrong answers, ⏭️ Skip a question, or ⚡ Double your points!
