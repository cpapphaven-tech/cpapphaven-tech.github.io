📝 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  1. How do I add a new expense?
    Tap the Add Expense button, enter the amount, category, and optional note, then save.
  2. Where is my data stored?
    All data is stored locally on your device only. No information is uploaded to any server.
  3. Do I need an account or login?
    No — the app works without login. You can start tracking expenses instantly.
  4. Can I use the app offline?
    Yes. The app works completely offline, anytime, anywhere.
  5. Will my data be shared?
    Never. We do not collect or share your personal information.

🔐 Privacy & Security

Your data stays only on your device.

We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage and improve features.

We use Firebase Crashlytics to fix bugs and crashes.

No personal data is collected. No tracking or advertising profiles.
