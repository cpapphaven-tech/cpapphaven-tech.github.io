    How to Play Family Tree!

    Put your logic and deductive reasoning to the test:

    • Read the Clues: Carefully analyze the hints provided. They will tell you relationships, genders, or exact positions.
    • Select and Place: Tap a character portrait at the bottom to select them, then tap an empty slot in the tree to assign them.
    • Correct Mistakes: Tap an already placed character in the tree to return them to the bank.
    • Level Up: Successfully completing a tree unlocks the next, more complex family puzzle.

    About the Game

    Family Tree! Logic Puzzles is a brain-training game that challenges your critical thinking. Reconstruct the lineage of generations, piece together the word clues, and watch your IQ soar as the trees grow more intricate.
