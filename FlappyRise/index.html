How to Play Flappy Rise

Experience the ultimate test of patience and timing:

  • Flapping: Click your mouse, tap your screen, or press the spacebar to flap your wings and rise.
  • Gravity: Gravity is always pulling you down. You must time your flaps to maintain the perfect altitude.
  • Obstacles: Navigate through the gaps in the green pipes. Hitting a pipe or touching the ground will end your flight.
  • Difficulty: The game gets faster every 5 points you score. Stay focused!

About the Game

Flappy Rise is a modern tribute to the legendary arcade mechanics that took the world by storm. Built with the Phaser engine, it offers smooth physics and responsive controls optimized for both desktop and mobile devices.

Our version features a dynamic difficulty scaling system and custom particle effects to make every flight feel immersive and premium.
