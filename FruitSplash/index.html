Fruit Splash – Free Online Puzzle Game

Play free online Fruit Splash game. Swap adjacent fruits to form vertical or horizontal lines of three or more matching fruits. Every match boosts your score and drops new fruits from above! Challenge yourself to reach higher levels and face bigger targets.

How to Play

Tap and drag a fruit towards an adjacent fruit to swap them. If the swap creates a match of 3 or more identical fruits, they will pop, and you'll earn points. If the swap doesn't result in a match, the fruits will return to their original positions.
