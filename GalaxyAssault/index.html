How to Play

Aliens are racing down the neon highway — stop them all:

  • Desktop: Move your mouse to steer the ship. Click (or hold) to fire laser blasts.
  • Mobile: Drag your finger to steer. Tap to shoot.
  • Goal: Every enemy in the wave must be destroyed. Let even ONE reach the bottom → Game Over!
  • Waves: After clearing a wave, the next wave has more enemies coming faster. How far can you go?
  • Tip: Shoot MECH 🤖 and DEMON 👹 enemies early — they take multiple hits to kill!

About Alien Highway

Play free online Alien Highway. Inspired by the top-rated arcade shooters like Alien Shooter and Galaxy Attack, this is a fast, no-nonsense alien blaster where every enemy counts. Let one slip through and the mission is over!

Features wave-based alien formations, 5 enemy types, mouse/touch aim-and-fire, neon highway 3D perspective, particle explosions, and relentless pressure — always one mistake from defeat.

Original Space Invaders mechanics inspired by open-source project by Jawad Khan (MIT License). Redesigned with 3D perspective, new enemy types, mouse/touch controls, and Playmix visual style.
