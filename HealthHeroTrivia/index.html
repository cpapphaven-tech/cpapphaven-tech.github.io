How to Play Health Hero Trivia

  • Choose Your Answer: Tap one of the four options to answer the scenario or fact.
  • Read the Takeaway: Whether you're right or wrong, read the 💡 Healthy Takeaway at the bottom to learn something new!
  • Timer: You have 30 seconds to answer before time runs out.
  • Level Up: Answer questions correctly to level up and increase your rank from Rookie to Health Master!
  • Use Lifelines: Need help? Use ✂️ 50:50, ⏭️ Skip, or ⚡ Double your points.

Frequently Asked Questions

What topics are covered in the Health Hero Quiz?

We cover 4 main pillars of daily wellness: Nutrition facts, Sleep Hygiene practices, First-Aid basics, and Vitamins. It acts as an excellent first aid practice online and nutrition game.

Is the information in the game medically certified?

The takeaways are based on widely accepted general health and wellness guidelines (like recommended daily water intake, CPR ratios). However, it is an educational game, not a substitute for professional medical advice.

Can kids play this game?

Yes! It is highly beneficial for students and children to learn basic nutrition and first aid from an early age in a fun, point-based system.

Do I need to sign up to play?

No registration is required. You can start playing the health and wellness quiz instantly from any browser on desktop or mobile.
