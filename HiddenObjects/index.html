🔍 About Hidden Objects

Hidden Objects - Mystery Finder is a classic item finding game designed to test your observational skills and visual acuity. Walk through different worlds like the cozy Toy Shop, the deep and mysterious Enchanted Jungle, and the mechanic's Retro Garage. Each level contains items cleverly disguised or placed in harmony with the environment. Take your time, focus your eyes, and enjoy a calming yet engaging puzzle experience.

🎮 How to Play

  • ✅ Look at the target list at the bottom of the screen.
  • ✅ Scan the scene to find matching objects.
  • ✅ Tap/click the objects in the picture to collect them.
  • ✅ Avoid random clicking; it will deduct time from your timer!
  • ✅ Use the Hint button when you cannot find the last few items.
  • ✅ Clear the level by finding all items before time runs out.
