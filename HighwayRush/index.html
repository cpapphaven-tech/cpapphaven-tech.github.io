Highway Rush: Traffic Dodge

Prepare for the ultimate road challenge. Highway Rush: Traffic Dodge is a high-speed arcade game where you navigate a busy highway. Dodge traffic cones, avoid obstacles, and keep your red sports car on the asphalt to set the highest possible distance record.

🛣️ Road Theme
🏎️ Sports Car
🚧 Traffic Dodge
🏁 High Score
🕹️ Arcade Classic

Game Instructions

  • Pilot: Drag your finger or mouse to steer the red car.
  • Avoid: Stay clear of orange cones and blue traffic vehicles.
  • Goal: Drive as far as possible without crashing!
