💎 About Jewel Sort: Sparkle Color Puzzle

Jewel Sort is a relaxing yet strategic color puzzle game. Drop sparkling precious gemstones from trays above, watch them land on the ever-moving circular conveyor belt, and guide them into the matching color treasure crates below. With only 3 slots per crate, every drop counts!

As levels progress, more colors and trays are introduced, making the puzzle increasingly tricky. Calm ASMR-style physics and sparkling jewel animations make this the perfect stress-relief game.

🎮 How to Play

  • 💎 Tap a tray at the top to drop a gemstone onto the conveyor
  • 🔄 Conveyor keeps moving – plan ahead before dropping!
  • 📦 Auto-Pack: Gemstones will automatically drop into matching color crates as they pass underneath
  • ✅ Complete crates – fill all 3 slots with the same color
  • ⚠️ Don't overfill – if belt is full with no matches, you lose!

🏆 Tips & Tricks

  • 📌 Check which crates are partially filled before dropping gemstones
  • 🧠 Think 2-3 moves ahead – the belt moves constantly
  • ⚡ Drop gemstones strategically to avoid overflowing the belt
  • 🎯 Try to complete a crate efficiently to earn bonus points
