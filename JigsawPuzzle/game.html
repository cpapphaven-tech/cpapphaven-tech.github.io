🧩 About Jigsaw Puzzle Royale

Assemble interlocking shapes of high-definition cosmic scenes, fantasy woodlands, glowing retro city grids, and adorable animal faces. Jigsaw Puzzle Royale delivers a relaxing yet highly satisfying mechanical layout featuring automatic piece snapping, 3D piece shadows, and custom-sliced vector tabs.

Adjust your challenge from a quick 9-piece grid up to a highly challenging 25-piece grid. Play completely offline with high-fidelity canvas procedurally generated fallback scenes, or connect online to pull vivid high-resolution graphics.

🎮 How to Play

  • 🎯 Select a category and grid size to generate your custom puzzle.
  • 📦 Tap any piece in the tray container at the bottom to select it.
  • 🖱️ Drag on the board to move the selected piece.
  • ⚡ Snap: Position the piece close to its original target location. When close enough, it will automatically snap lock into place.
  • 💡 Hint: Use the Hint button to briefly overlay a faint template of the completed puzzle image on the board.

🏆 Tips for Success

  • 🔍 Try activating the Hint mode if you're unsure of where a specific detail lies.
  • 🚀 Place edge pieces first. They have flat borders on at least one edge, making them easier to position.
  • ⏰ Speed counts! Complete the puzzle faster to maximize your high score bonus.
