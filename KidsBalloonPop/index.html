🎈 About Kids Balloon Pop

A classic balloon pop baby learning game with colorful graphics! Designed for toddlers aged 2–5. Tap to pop balloons and help your child learn the alphabet, numbers, animals, colors and shapes — in 12 languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Hindi, Tamil, Gujarati, Telugu, Marathi, Kannada, and Malayalam!

🎮 4 Learning Modes

  • A – Z: Learn letters of the alphabet & phonics.
  • 1 – 20: Pop balloons and learn counting numbers.
  • Colors: Popping balloons of different colors.
  • Shapes: Explore squares, triangles, circles and more!

🌍 12 Languages Supported

All 4 game modes include spoken audio labels and on-balloon text in English 🇬🇧, Spanish 🇪🇸, French 🇫🇷, German 🇩🇪, Japanese 🇯🇵, Hindi 🇮🇳, Tamil 🇮🇳, Gujarati 🇮🇳, Telugu 🇮🇳, Marathi 🇮🇳, Kannada 🇮🇳, and Malayalam 🇮🇳. Just tap the 🌍 button to switch languages any time!

❓ FAQ

Is it safe for toddlers?

Yes! The game is 100% ad-free during play, and no internet is needed after first load.

Who else can benefit?

Users with Alzheimer's, Parkinson's, dementia, autism, or CVI also find the game's simple, sensory-friendly interaction deeply enjoyable and therapeutic.
