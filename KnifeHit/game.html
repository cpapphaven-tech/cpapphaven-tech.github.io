🎯 About Knife Hit: Target Throw Arcade

Knife Hit is a thrilling reaction-based arcade game designed to test your reflexes and timing. Tap to throw sharp knives directly into the center rotating wooden targets. The target log spins at variable speeds and changes direction unexpectedly, demanding complete concentration and precise execution.

Your objective is to embed a specific number of knives into the log. If you throw a knife and it strikes another blade already embedded, the log will reject it and trigger a Game Over. Along the way, slash juicy apples to score extra points and set high score records.

🎮 How to Play

  • 🎯 Tap the screen to launch a knife straight upwards.
  • 🌀 Read the target: Anticipate the log's acceleration, deceleration, and random reverses.
  • 🍎 Slash apples: Target apples on the rotating wooden log for high-score bonuses.
  • 🚫 Avoid clashes: Never let a thrown knife collide with existing pins or knives on the log.
  • 🏆 Finish the set: Successfully stick all your available knives to shatter the log and advance!

🏆 Tips & Tricks

  • 📌 Wait for the pattern: Watch the log for one full rotation to learn its current speed cycle.
  • ⚡ Group them tight: Place your knives close to each other when the log is slow to save space for later throws.
  • 🧠 Stay calm: Panic-clicking leads to mistakes. Take your time to target open gaps.
  • 🎯 Expect the snap: In higher stages, logs will snap-reverse directions very quickly.
