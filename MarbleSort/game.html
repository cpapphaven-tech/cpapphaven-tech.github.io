🔮 About Marble Sort: Color Puzzle

Marble Sort is a relaxing yet strategic color puzzle game. Drop colorful marbles from trays above, watch them land on the ever-moving circular conveyor belt, and guide them into the matching color boxes below. With only 3 slots per box, every drop counts!

As levels progress, more colors and trays are introduced, making the puzzle increasingly tricky. Calm ASMR-style physics and smooth marble animations make this the perfect stress-relief game.

🎮 How to Play

  • 🔴 Tap a tray at the top to drop a marble onto the conveyor
  • 🔄 Conveyor keeps moving – plan ahead before dropping!
  • 📦 Tap a color box below to pack a matching marble from the belt
  • ✅ Complete boxes – fill all 3 slots with the same color
  • ⚠️ Don't overfill – if belt is full with no matches, you lose!

🏆 Tips & Tricks

  • 📌 Check which boxes are partially filled before dropping marbles
  • 🧠 Think 2-3 moves ahead – the belt moves constantly
  • ⚡ Pack boxes quickly to free up belt space
  • 🎯 Try to complete a box in one go to earn bonus points
