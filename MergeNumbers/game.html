About Merge Numbers 2248

Race against the clock! Each level gives you 60 seconds to reach a target score. Connect same-number tiles in any of 8 directions and merge them — release to combine them into a tile with double the value.

Unlimited levels — first 10 levels are easy to get you started. As levels increase, so does the target score. How far can you reach?

How to Play

  • Connect: Touch & drag through adjacent same-value tiles (8 directions).
  • Merge: Release to merge into one tile (value ×2).
  • Chain Bonus: Longer chains = more points!
  • Level Up: Hit the target score before 60 seconds run out.
