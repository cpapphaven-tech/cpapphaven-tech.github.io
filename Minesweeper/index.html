How to Play Minesweeper

  • Select a difficulty level: Easy (9x9 with 10 mines), Medium (16x16 with 40 mines), or Hard (16x30 with 99 mines).
  • Click or tap on any square to reveal it. The first click will always be safe!
  • The numbers displayed on revealed squares represent how many mines are adjacent to that square (horizontally, vertically, and diagonally).
  • Use the numbers to deduce where the hidden mines are located.
  • To place a flag on a suspected mine, switch to Flag Mode using the toggle button, or long-press the tile on mobile (right-click on desktop).
  • Reveal all non-mine squares to win the game! If you click on a mine, it will explode and the game will be over.

Features

  • Classic Windows Minesweeper rules with a beautiful, modern neon look.
  • First-Click Safety guarantee: You'll never hit a mine on your first turn.
  • Optimized for touchscreens: Mobile-friendly controls, including a dedicated flag toggle button and long-press actions.
