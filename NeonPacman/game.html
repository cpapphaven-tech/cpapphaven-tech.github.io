How to Play

Navigate the glowing neon grid with simple controls:

  • Desktop: Use Arrow Keys or WASD to guide your character.
  • Mobile: Swipe your finger anywhere on the screen to change direction.
  • Objective: Eat all the glowing neon pellets in the level without getting caught by the Cyber Ghosts.

About Neon Pacman Arcade

Experience the classic arcade chase in a stunning modern reboot! Neon Pacman Arcade takes the legendary maze-chomping gameplay you know and love and electrifies it with a vibrant, synthwave aesthetic, dynamic lighting, and smooth responsive controls.

Whether you are reliving the arcade glory days or experiencing the rush for the first time, Neon Pacman provides an addictive, high-score chasing experience right in your browser, for free, without any downloads.

Based on open-source Pac-Man mechanics by Talha (MIT License)

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the game free to play?

Yes, Neon Pacman Arcade is 100% free and playable directly in your web browser.

Can I play on my tablet or mobile phone?

Absolutely! The game is completely optimized for touch screens with a smooth swipe mechanic.

How do I increase my score?

Eat the small neon pellets for 10 points each, and seek out the large energy pearls for 50 points. Compete to get the highest score possible!
