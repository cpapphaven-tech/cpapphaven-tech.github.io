Game Information

Neon Slicer 3D

Slice the mathematical balloons flying up from the ground to reach your target score before time runs out! Pop positive numbers but avoid negative values.

How to Play

Swipe or click across the balloons to pop them! Positive values add to your score, while negative values deduct points. Reach the target score for each level within 60 seconds.

Scoring

  • Positive: +1 to +50 points
  • Negative: Deducts the face value
  • Combos: Slice multiple balloons in one swipe for bonus points!

FAQ

Are bigger numbers better? Yes! High numbers give you more score rapidly but exist in smaller quantities.
