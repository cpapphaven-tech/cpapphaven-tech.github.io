How to Play

Navigate the neon grid with your snake:

  • Controls (Desktop): The snake automatically turns toward your hovering mouse cursor. No clicking required!
  • Controls (Mobile): Swipe anywhere on the screen (Up, Down, Left, Right) to direct the snake.
  • Rules: Collect glowing orbs to gain points. You must reach the Target Score before the countdown timer hits zero to advance to the next level.
  • Beware: If you crash into the arena borders or bite your own glowing tail, it's game over!

About Neon Snake

Play free online Neon Snake game. An enhanced, level-based adaptation of the classic Snake arcade game featuring dynamic mouse-hover controls, swipe support for mobile, and a thrilling countdown element.

Based on original open-source Snake mechanics by Talha Bin Yousaf (MIT License). Re-imagined with modern Neon aesthetics, precision tracking, and progressive level design exclusively for Playmix Games.
