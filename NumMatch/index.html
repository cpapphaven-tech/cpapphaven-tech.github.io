🧩 About NumMatch - Logic Puzzle

NumMatch is the perfect relaxing number puzzle game. If you enjoy Sudoku, Ten Crush, Crossword puzzles, or classic number matching mechanics, you will love training your logic and concentration skills with this beautifully crafted web experience!

Immerse yourself in the world of math and pattern recognition. Solve the daily puzzles to train your brain, boost your math skills, and relax after a long day.

🎮 How to Play

  • The goal is to clear all numbers from the board.
  • Find pairs of identical numbers (e.g., 1 & 1, 7 & 7) or pairs that add up to 10 (e.g., 6 & 4, 3 & 7).
  • Pairs can be cleared vertically, horizontally, or diagonally—provided there are no other un-cleared numbers blocking their path.
  • You can also match the last number of a row with the first number of the next row.
  • When there are no more matches, press ➕ Add to generate extra numbers.
  • If you're ever stuck, use the 💡 Hint button to reveal a valid move.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is NumMatch free to play?
Yes! You can play NumMatch logic puzzle completely free directly in your web browser with no download required.

Does it work on mobile phones?
Absolutely. The grid is fully responsive and optimized for scrolling on touchscreens. A QR code is provided on desktop browsers so you can quickly switch to mobile!

How do I win?
You win a level when all numbers on the board have been cleared. Clearing entire rows at once is a great strategy as it shrinks the board.
