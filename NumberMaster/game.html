About Number Master: Run, Merge & Grow

Swipe left and right to move your blue number through an endless course. Collect green numbers to grow your value — they are always ≤ your current number so they're always safe to absorb.

Avoid red threat numbers — they stay dangerous forever and will drain your value and score if you hit them. Purple minus numbers also subtract from your progress. Saws end the game instantly!

At the end of each level, break through walls using your accumulated number. The TARGET in the HUD shows the total wall value you need to clear the level.

How to Play

  • Swipe: Move left or right to navigate the lanes.
  • 🟢 Green: Collect these to grow your number value.
  • 🔴 Red: Avoid these — they steal your value and score.
  • 🟣 Purple: Avoid these minus numbers too.
  • ⚙️ Saws: Game over on contact.
  • 🧱 Walls: At the goal, break them if your number is big enough!
