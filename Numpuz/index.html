🧩 About Numpuz: Number Puzzle Games

Numpuz is one of the most beloved classic puzzle brain games. Tap and move the wood number blocks, enjoy the magic of logic puzzles, coordinate your eyes, hands and brain. Challenge your logic and brainpower, have fun and enjoy number games!

🎮 How to Play Numpuz?

Numpuz puzzle brain games consist of a frame of number blocks in random order, with one tile missing. The object of the logic puzzles are to place the number blocks in order by making sliding moves that use the empty space. Endless challenge of puzzle brain games that will test your logical thinking and iq limits.
