About Odd One Out

Odd One Out is a fast-paced brain puzzle game that challenges your pattern recognition and visual perception. Each round presents a grid of emojis, colors, or shapes — and one sneaky item doesn't belong!

How to Play

  • Scan the grid: Quickly identify the one item that's different from all others.
  • Tap it: Touch the odd one out before time runs out.
  • Build streaks: Consecutive correct answers multiply your points!
  • Be careful: Wrong taps cost you 5 precious seconds.

Game Modes & Difficulty

The game features 12 progressive levels with three puzzle types: Emoji (find the unrelated emoji), Color (spot the different shade), and Shape (identify the mismatched geometric form). Grids grow from a 2×2 up to a 5×5 challenge.

Scoring

Each correct answer earns 10 points. Build a streak to earn bonus points — a 5× streak earns 30 points per correct answer! Wrong answers deduct points and reduce your time, so accuracy matters.
