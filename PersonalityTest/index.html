Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the test take?

Most users complete the 2-choice personality test in about 60–90 seconds. It's designed to be fast and intuitive.

What are the 5 personality types?

The types are Strategist (planner), Action Taker (impulsive), Analyzer (logical), Creative (imaginative), and Chill Player (relaxed).

Can I retake the quiz?

Absolutely! If you feel like your mood has changed or you want to see other types, just hit the "Try Again" button.

Is this a real psychological test?

It's a fun, engaging quiz designed for entertainment and game discovery. While it reflects real traits, it's meant to be a lighthearted way to explore your preferences.
