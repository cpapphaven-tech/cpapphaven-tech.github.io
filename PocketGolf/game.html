About Pocket Golf vs AI

Play free online Pocket Golf vs AI game. Pocket Golf vs AI is a precision-based mini-golf puzzle game. Navigate through challenging levels filled with obstacles, narrow corridors, and dangerous spikes. Compete head-to-head against a smart AI opponent where strategy and precision are key to victory.

Game Rules

  • Objective: Sink the ball in the hole with fewer strokes than the AI.
  • Turn System: Players alternate turns. You shoot, then the AI shoots.
  • Shot Limit: You have a maximum of 3 strokes per hole. Failing to sink the ball in 3 results in a penalty.
  • Scoring: Hole in 1 = 3pts, Hole in 2 = 2pts, Hole in 3 = 1pt.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I aim?

Touch the golf ball and drag in the opposite direction you want to shoot. A power line will show you the strength and direction.

What happens if I hit spikes?

Hitting spikes will reset the ball to its previous position and add a penalty stroke.

Is the game mobile-friendly?

Yes! Pocket Golf is designed to work perfectly on all mobile devices and tablets.
