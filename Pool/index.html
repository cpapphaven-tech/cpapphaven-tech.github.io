How to Play

Classic 8-Ball Pool rules with intuitive mouse and touch controls:

  • Aim: Move your mouse or drag around the cue ball to aim the stick.
  • Shoot: Click and drag backwards to pull the stick and increase power. Release to shoot!
  • Rules: First player sinks a stripe or solid ball to claim that suit. Sink all your balls, then the 8-ball to win.
  • Fouls: Sinking the cue ball or opponent's ball results in a foul and gives the other player ball-in-hand.

About Pool Master

Play free online Pool Master — a beautifully redesigned 8-ball pool experience built for PlayMix. Features realistic physics, single player AI, local multiplayer, and complete dragging controls.
