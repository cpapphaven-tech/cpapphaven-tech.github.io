⛷️ SkiFree Online – Play the Classic Retro Game

Welcome to SkiFree Classic! Relive the nostalgia of the iconic 1991 Windows game directly in your browser. Steer your skier down the infinite snowy slopes, avoid trees, rocks, dogs, and other obstacles.

Hit ramps for sick aerial jumps and try to cover as much distance as possible. But watch out—once you go far enough, the infamous Abominable Snow Monster will come chasing after you. Can you outrun him?

🎮 How to Play

  • Controls: Use the Arrow Keys to steer left, right, up (slow down), and down (speed up).
  • Jumping: Hit ramps to fly over obstacles.
  • Mobile/Touch: Tap and hold the sides of the screen or swipe to guide your skier.
  • The Yeti: The Yeti runs faster than you! Use jumps and precise turns to outmaneuver him as long as you can.
