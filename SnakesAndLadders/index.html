How to Play

Classic Snakes & Ladders rules with smooth graphics:

  • Roll: Click the "ROLL DICE" button to roll the 3D dice.
  • Movement: Your token moves forward step-by-step by the value rolled.
  • Ladders: Land on the bottom of a ladder to climb up to its top.
  • Snakes: Land on the head of a snake and you'll slide down to its tail.
  • Roll 6 Bonus: Roll a 6 to get a bonus roll! Be careful, a maximum of two consecutive 6s gives you bonus turns.
  • Goal: Reach exactly Tile 100 to win the match. If your roll is too high, your token bounces back from 100!

About Snakes and Ladders

Enjoy the timeless board game classic, also known as Chutes and Ladders or Ludo's companion. Beautifully designed for PlayMix, this game features an animated canvas-rendered board with curved detailed snakes, shaded ladders, interactive 3D dice rolling physics, and custom audio synthesis.

Fully optimized for mobile touchscreens, tablets, and desktop displays with responsive aspect ratio locking.
