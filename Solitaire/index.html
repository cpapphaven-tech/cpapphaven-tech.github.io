How to Play Klondike Solitaire

  • The goal is to build up the four foundations by suit from Ace to King.
  • In the tableau, you can build cards down by alternating colors.
  • Only a King can be placed on an empty tableau space.
  • Use the stock pile to draw additional cards when you get stuck.

Features

  • Classic Klondike gameplay with smooth drag-and-drop.
  • Auto-move feature to speed up the game.
  • Unlimited Undo to fix your mistakes.
  • Mobile and desktop optimized UI.
