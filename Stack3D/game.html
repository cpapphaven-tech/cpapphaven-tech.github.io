      Master Your Timing

      Stack 3D is a minimalist game that tests your precision and reflexes. Build a tower as high as you can by perfectly timing your taps. Simple to learn, yet addictive and challenging to master.

      How to Stack Higher

      Wait for the block to align with the tower and tap. Any parts that overhang will be cut off, making the next stack harder. Achieve multiple "Perfect" placements in a row to expand the stacking area and score bonus points!
