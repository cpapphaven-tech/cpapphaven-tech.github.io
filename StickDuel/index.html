Features of Stickman Neon Warriors

  • Physics-based stickman combat
  • Advanced AI for challenging 1v1 duels
  • Neon-drenched cyber-pyramid atmosphere
  • Hoverboards and multiple platform layouts
  • Optimized for both desktop and mobile touch

How to Play

Control your stickman warrior and aim for the opponent's weak spots. Use the platforms to your advantage and avoid the lava at all costs!

  • Desktop: Arrows to move, Up to jump, F or Shift to shoot.
  • Mobile: Use on-screen D-pad and action buttons.
