🍣 About Sushi Match 3D

Play free online Sushi Match 3D game. Sushi Match 3D is an addictive memory matching game where you click on pairs of matching sushi to clear the board. Test your memory and speed in beautiful 3D environments inspired by traditional Japanese cuisine. Perfect for players of all ages looking for a relaxing yet challenging puzzle experience.

🎮 How to Play

  • ✅ Click on sushi items to flip and find matching pairs
  • ✅ Match all pairs before the timer runs out
  • ✅ Complete levels to unlock new sushi varieties
  • ✅ Earn bonus points for fast clears
  • ✅ Challenge yourself with increasing difficulty levels
