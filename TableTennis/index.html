🏓 About Table Tennis World Tour

Step up to the table and take on the AI in this fast-paced, realistic Table Tennis World Tour game! Experience the thrill of competitive ping pong with smooth physics, intelligent AI opponents, and a stunning 3D perspective view of the table. Control your paddle to serve, rally, and smash your way to victory!

🎮 How to Play

  • ✅ Move your mouse or drag your finger to control the paddle
  • ✅ Hit the ball back over the net before it bounces twice on your side
  • ✅ The first player to reach 11 points wins the set
  • ✅ Win 3 sets to claim the match victory
  • ✅ Use angled shots to outsmart the AI

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Table Tennis World Tour free?
Yes, completely free with no downloads or sign-ups required.

Does it work on mobile?
Yes, the game is fully optimized for touchscreens on phones and tablets.

How hard is the AI?
The AI adapts its speed and accuracy as you win points, providing a progressively challenging experience.
