How to Play

Master the court with these simple steps:

  • Movement: Drag your racket (mouse/touch) anywhere in the lower half of the court.
  • Positioning: Move forward toward the net to cut off angles, or stay back to defend powerful shots.
  • Hitting: The ball will automatically bounce off your racket. Your position and speed determine the trajectory.
  • Winning: Outsmart the AI by hitting the ball where it can't reach. First to 15 points wins!

About Arcade Tennis

Experience the thrill of classic arcade-style tennis with modern physics and smooth 2D graphics. This game brings the classic "Pong" mechanic to the next level with character positioning, depth strategy, and a dynamically scaling AI opponent.

Designed for both mobile and desktop, Arcade Tennis Champion offers a fast-paced challenge that rewards quick reflexes and strategic placement. Whether you're a casual player or a sports gaming enthusiast, the court is waiting for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the game free to play?

Yes, Arcade Tennis Champion is 100% free and playable directly in your web browser.

How do I increase my score?

You score a point whenever the ball passes the AI's baseline. Try hitting the ball at sharp angles to make it harder for the AI to return.

Can I play on my phone?

Absolutely! The game is optimized for touch controls and works seamlessly on all modern smartphones and tablets.

Is there a 2-player mode?

Currently, the game features a single-player mode against an AI opponent. We are constantly improving our games based on user feedback!
