About Traffic Jam

Traffic Jam is a classic spatial reasoning puzzle where you take the role of a traffic controller in a congested city parking lot. Your goal is simple but challenging: get the red car to the exit gate on the right side of the grid. However, the path is blocked by various trucks and cars that can only move along their own axis.

With dozens of levels ranging from "Beginner" to "Expert", Traffic Jam will test your ability to plan several moves ahead. Each car you move affects the board's state, creating a domino effect of possibilities. It's a perfect blend of strategy and logic, rendered in high-fidelity 3D with smooth isometric camera angles.

Rules of the Road

  • Horizontal Cars: Can only slide left and right.
  • Vertical Cars: Can only slide up and down.
  • The Exit: Slide the Red Car all the way to the green gate on the right to win.
  • No Jumping: Cars cannot jump over or pass through other vehicles.
