💧 About Water Sort 3D

Play free online Water Sort 3D game. Water Sort 3D is a relaxing color-sorting puzzle game inspired by the viral Water Sort Puzzle. Pour water between tubes to organize colors and complete challenging levels. With hundreds of levels and increasingly complex puzzles, this game offers endless brain-teasing fun for all ages. Perfect for unwinding while exercising your logic skills.

🎮 How to Play

  • ✅ Tap tubes to pour water between containers
  • ✅ Sort colors so each tube contains only one color
  • ✅ Use empty tubes to help organize and separate colors
  • ✅ Avoid mixing colors incorrectly or use undo moves
  • ✅ Complete all levels to master the water sorting challenge
