Master the Modern Word Crossword

Step into the ultimate word challenge with Word Crossword! This addictive puzzle game combines the thrill of anagrams with the logic of crossword puzzles. Challenge your vocabulary, sharpen your mind, and discover hidden words as you connect letters in a beautiful, modern interface.

How to Play

  • Connect: Click/Touch and drag between letters in the circle to form a word.
  • Solve: Correct words will automatically fill their spots in the crossword grid above.
  • Bonus: Find extra hidden words that aren't in the grid to earn bonus coins!
  • Hints: Use your earned coins to buy hints when you're stuck on a tough level.
