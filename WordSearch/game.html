🔍 About Word Search

Play free online Word Search game. Word Search is a classic word puzzle game where you find hidden words in a grid of letters arranged horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Perfect for improving vocabulary and concentration, Word Search offers relaxing gameplay for all ages. With themed puzzles ranging from animals to geography, science to pop culture, there's always a new challenge to discover.

🎮 How to Play

  • ✅ Find all hidden words in the grid
  • ✅ Words can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal
  • ✅ Words can read forward or backward
  • ✅ Click or drag to highlight found words
  • ✅ Complete the puzzle to find all words in the list
