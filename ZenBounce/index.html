About Zen Bounce Puzzle

Zen Bounce Puzzle is a minimalist physics-based puzzle game. Your goal is to clear the board of all white blocks by launching a ball. Calculate the perfect angles, let the ball bounce around, and enjoy the satisfying sounds of clearing a level.

Game Rules

  • Objective: The ball must touch all white targets to clear the level.
  • Obstacles: Black blocks act as walls for the ball to bounce off but do not need to be cleared.
  • One Shot: You only get one shot per attempt. If the ball leaves the screen without hitting all white targets, you must restart the level.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

Touch and drag anywhere on the screen to draw an aiming line. Release to shoot the ball in that direction.

What if I get stuck?

Experiment with different angles! Sometimes bouncing the ball off the edge of the screen or an obstacle is the key to victory.
