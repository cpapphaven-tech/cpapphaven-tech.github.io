Color Twist – Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when using the Color Twist mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Firebase Analytics

The app uses Google Firebase Analytics to understand basic usage patterns. Firebase may automatically collect:

  • App interactions (screens you visit)
  • Device type (model, OS version)
  • Country (approximate, non‑precise)
  • App performance data

Firebase does not collect personal identity information such as your name, email, or phone number.

Firebase uses a unique installation ID that is not linked to your real identity.

1.2 Firebase Crashlytics

To improve app stability, Crashlytics collects:

  • Crash logs
  • Device information at the time of crash
  • Minimal diagnostics

Crash reports do not contain personal data and are used only to fix bugs.

2. No Personal Information Collected

The app does not collect:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Phone
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Precise location
  • Address
  • Payment data

We do not create accounts or track user identity.

3. How We Use Data

Data collected is used only for:

  • Improving app features
  • Fixing crashes and bugs
  • Understanding general usage patterns

We do not sell, rent, or share data with any third party beyond Firebase (Google).

4. Children's Privacy

Color Twist is kid‑friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children under 13.

5. Third‑Party Services

This app uses the following third‑party services:

  • Google Firebase Analytics
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google AdMob (for ads)

6. Advertising (AdMob)

Color Twist displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain non‑personal device and usage information required for delivering ads and preventing fraud.

We do not use advertising identifiers to track users across apps.

7. Internet Access

Internet is used only for:

  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Displaying AdMob ads

No other data is sent online.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect app improvements. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
