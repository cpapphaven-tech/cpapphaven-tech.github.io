Troubleshooting

  • No sound? Make sure your device volume is up and the app's mute switch (if any) is off.
  • Ads not showing? Ads may not show in development unless using test IDs, or if the device has no network. In production, ad fill depends on regional availability.
  • App crashes? Please send crash logs if available. We use Crashlytics to gather automatic crash reports.
