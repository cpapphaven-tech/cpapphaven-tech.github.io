Privacy Policy

App: PlayMix Games: Offline

1. Summary

PlayMix Games: Offline is a multi-game collection that includes a variety of puzzle, casual, action, arcade, quiz, and reflex-based games. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

The app uses third-party services for analytics, crash reporting, and advertising, such as Firebase, Google Mobile Ads (AdMob), and ironSource (by Unity).

2. Personal data we collect

PlayMix Games does not require users to create an account and does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or payment details by default.

If you contact us for support (for example, by email), the message may include personal information that you voluntarily provide. This information is used only to respond to your request.

3. Device and usage data

To operate and improve the app, certain device and usage information may be collected automatically, including:

  • Device model, operating system version, and language settings
  • App version, session duration, and gameplay events
  • Crash reports and performance metrics
  • Approximate location derived from IP address (city-level)
  • Advertising identifiers (such as IDFA on iOS)

This data is generally collected in aggregated form and is used to improve performance, stability, and overall gameplay experience.

4. Advertising (Google Mobile Ads / AdMob, ironSource)

PlayMix Games displays ads using Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). AdMob and its partners may collect and process certain data to show ads, measure performance, and prevent fraud.

This data may include:

  • Advertising identifiers (such as IDFA)
  • Device and app usage information
  • Ad interaction data (impressions and clicks)
  • Approximate location (city-level)

You can control ad personalization through your device settings. For more information, please refer to Google’s privacy policies.

In compliance with Apple's AppTrackingTransparency framework, the app may request permission to track users for advertising purposes. If permission is denied, only non-personalized ads will be shown.

PlayMix Games may also use ironSource (a Unity company) to serve and mediate advertisements. ironSource and its advertising partners may collect and process certain data to display ads, measure performance, and prevent fraud.

This data may include (ironSource) :

  • Advertising identifiers (such as IDFA)
  • Device and app usage information
  • Ad interaction data (impressions and clicks)
  • Approximate location (city-level)

ironSource may use this information in accordance with its own privacy policy.

5. Analytics and crash reporting

PlayMix Games may use analytics and crash reporting tools, such as Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics, to understand how the app is used and to improve reliability.

The data collected by these services is generally aggregated and is not used to identify individual users.

6. Where data is stored

Game progress and local settings are stored on your device. PlayMix Games does not upload gameplay data to its own servers.

Third-party services (analytics and advertising providers) may store and process data on their own servers.

7. Children

PlayMix Games is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

8. Third-party links and services

The app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We do not control the privacy practices of these third parties and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

9. Data retention

Analytics and advertising-related data is retained only as long as necessary for performance analysis, advertising measurement, fraud prevention, or legal requirements. Aggregated or anonymized data may be retained for longer periods.

10. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

11. International data transfers

Data collected by third-party service providers may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your region. By using the app, you consent to such transfers as permitted by law.

12. No Purchases or Currency

PlayMix Games does not include in-app purchases, subscriptions, or any form of virtual or real-world currency. All scores and rewards shown in the app are for gameplay purposes only and have no real-world value. Scores reset each game session and are used only during gameplay. Progression through levels occurs automatically through normal gameplay and does not involve purchases or currency.

13. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted within the app or on this page. Continued use of the app after changes means acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact
