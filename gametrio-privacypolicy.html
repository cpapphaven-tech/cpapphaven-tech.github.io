Privacy Policy

App: Puzzle Trio

Primary language: English (U.S.)

1. Summary

Puzzle Trio bundles three games in one app: Sudoku, Lane Runner (car runner), and Word Puzzle. This Privacy Policy explains what (if any) information the app collects, how it is used, and your choices. The app uses third-party services for analytics, crash reporting, and advertising (for example, Firebase and Google Mobile Ads / AdMob).

2. Personal data we collect

The app does not require you to create an account and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or payment details by default. Any feedback or support messages you send (for example via an in-app contact form or email) may include the contact details you voluntarily provide.

3. Device and usage data

To operate and improve the app we may automatically collect certain device and usage information, including:

  • Device model, OS version and language settings;
  • App version, session and gameplay events (screens viewed, feature use, scores);
  • Crash reports and performance metrics;
  • Approximate location derived from IP (city level) for analytics and advertising;
  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., IDFA on iOS) used by ad networks.

This information is generally collected in aggregate and is used to improve the app and measure performance.

4. Advertising (Google Mobile Ads / AdMob)

Puzzle Trio displays ads using Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). AdMob and other advertising partners may collect and process certain information to deliver and measure ads, prevent fraud, and provide analytics. This may include:

  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., IDFA on iOS);
  • Device and app usage data;
  • Interaction data with ads (impressions, clicks);
  • Approximate location (city-level).

To learn more about Google's data use and options for controlling ad personalization, please visit Google's privacy pages.

5. Analytics and crash reporting

We may use analytics and crash reporting services (for example, Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics) to understand app usage and improve stability. These services collect device and usage data as described in Section 3. Data collected for analytics is generally aggregated and not tied to a specific person's identity.

6. Where data is stored

Game progress and local settings are stored on the user's device. We do not upload your gameplay data to our servers unless you explicitly choose to use a cloud or backup feature (if/when provided). Third-party services (analytics, ads) may store and process data on their servers.

7. Children

Puzzle Trio is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child provided personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

8. Your choices

You can control advertising personalization and the advertising identifier at the device level:

  • iOS: Use the Advertising & Privacy settings (Limit Ad Tracking / Reset Advertising Identifier) or turn off personalized ads.
  • Android: Manage Ads Personalization in Google settings or reset your advertising ID.

To remove game data, uninstall the app or delete saved progress within the app (if that feature is available).

9. Third-party links and services

The app may link to third-party websites or include content from third parties. We do not control third parties’ data practices; please review their privacy policies for details.

10. Data retention

We retain collected analytics and ad-related data for as long as needed for analytics, advertising measurement, fraud detection, or as required by law. Aggregated or anonymized data may be retained indefinitely.

11. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

12. International transfers

Data collected by third-party providers may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your jurisdiction. By using the app, you consent to such transfers.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will post changes within the app or on our website and update the effective date if applicable. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
