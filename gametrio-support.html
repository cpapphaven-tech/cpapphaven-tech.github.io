Welcome! Brain Mix: Sudoku, Runner,Word includes Sudoku, Lane Runner, and Word Puzzle. If you're experiencing an issue or have questions, you're in the right place.

📌 Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I play Sudoku?

Select a number and tap a cell to fill it. Use notes, hints, and undo options to help solve the puzzle.

2. How does Lane Runner work?

Swipe left or right to move your car between lanes. Collect stars and avoid obstacles to score higher.

3. How do I play Word Puzzle?

Swipe across letters to form valid words. Find all required words to complete the level.

4. Do the games save my progress?

Yes! All progress (Sudoku states, scores, and word puzzles) is stored locally on your device.

5. Why am I seeing ads?

Brain Mix: Sudoku, Runner,Word is free to play and uses Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to support development. We aim to keep ads minimal and non-intrusive.

🔧 Troubleshooting

Game running slow?

  • Restart the app
  • Close unused apps running in the background
  • Ensure your device has adequate storage

Ads showing too frequently?

Ads should appear in balanced intervals. If you feel it's excessive, please reach out so we can investigate.

Game progress not saving?

Brain Mix: Sudoku, Runner,Word stores progress on your device. Issues may occur if the app is force-closed or if storage is full. Restarting the device may help.
