📌 Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I add a new habit?

Tap the “+ Add Habit” button, enter the habit name, goal, optional reminder, and save.

2. How do I mark a habit as completed?

Tap the checkmark / Tap! button beside the habit. Your streak and progress update instantly.

3. How do reminders work?

Reminders are optional. If enabled, you'll receive a daily notification encouraging you to complete your habit.

4. My streak is not updating—why?

Streak increases only when you complete today's habit and haven't missed yesterday. Missing a day resets the streak.

🔧 Troubleshooting

Ads appearing too frequently

Ads help keep the app free. If the frequency feels high, please contact us—we value your feedback.

Notifications not showing?

  • Settings → Notifications → Habit Tracker → Enable
  • Ensure your device is updated
  • Restart your phone if needed

App not saving habits?

This can occur if your device is low on storage or the app was force-closed. Try restarting the app.
