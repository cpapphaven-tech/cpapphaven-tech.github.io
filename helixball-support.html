About the App

Ball Games: Helix & Dash includes two exciting ball games in one app:

  • Helix Drop – Control the ball using a joystick and swipe gestures to guide it downward through helix platforms.
  • Ice Ball Dash – Run, jump, double jump, and collect coins while avoiding obstacles on an icy road.
