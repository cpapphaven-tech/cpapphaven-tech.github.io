📘 FAQ

  1. The app shows the wrong answer or I can’t guess the logo.
    Try using Emoji Hint, AI Hint, or Voice Play modes for extra help.
  2. The app is not loading or freezing.
    Close the app and reopen it. If the problem continues, restart your device.
  3. I found a bug — how do I report it?
    Email us with the subject "Bug Report" and include screenshots if possible.
  4. Why can’t I find some brand logos?
    The app uses open-source icons only, not copyrighted brand images.

🔐 Privacy & Data

Logo Quiz does not collect personal data. The app only uses:

  • Firebase Crashlytics (to fix crashes)
  • Firebase Analytics (to understand app usage)
