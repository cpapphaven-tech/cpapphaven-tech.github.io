Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 24, 2026

1. Information Recovery

PlayMixGames does not require user registration. We do not collect personal information such as names, addresses, or phone numbers. However, we may use third-party services that collect data to identify you (e.g., Google AdSense).

2. Cookies

Cookies are files with a small amount of data that are commonly used as anonymous unique identifiers. These are sent to your browser from the websites that you visit and are stored on your device's internal memory. This service does not use these "cookies" explicitly. However, the app may use third-party code and libraries that use "cookies" to collect information and improve their services.

3. Advertising

We use Google AdSense to serve ads on our website. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@playmixgames.com.
