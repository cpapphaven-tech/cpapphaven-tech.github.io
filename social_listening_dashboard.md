# Playmix Facebook Social Listening Dashboard

Welcome to your Social Listening Dashboard! Here you will find highly personalized draft replies generated by Antigravity recommending Playmix games to people on Facebook who are looking for game recommendations.

## How to Use:
1. Review the generated draft replies below.
2. If you like a reply, click the **Post Link**, copy the **Drafted Response**, and paste it as a comment or reply on Facebook.
3. Check the checkbox next to the post to mark it as processed:
   - `[x] Approved & Posted`
   - `[~] Rejected / Skipped`
