Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled when you use our mobile application “Stack & Match 3D: Arcade Build”, which includes the games Stack 3D and Cube Match 3D. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to maintaining transparency regarding how data is used.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store any personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Home address
  • Password or login data

The app does not require creating an account and does not track personal identity.

2. Information Automatically Collected by Third Parties

Our app integrates third-party services that may collect technical and usage information automatically. This information is non-personal and used for analytics, advertising performance, and crash diagnostics.

Services We Use

  • Google AdMob – provides advertisements
  • Google Firebase Analytics – monitors gameplay behavior and diagnostics
  • Firebase Crashlytics – identifies and helps fix app crashes

These services may collect:

  • Device type, model, OS version
  • IP address (for regional relevance)
  • Anonymous usage statistics
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Crash logs and diagnostics

None of this information identifies you personally. Use of these services is governed by their respective privacy policies.

3. How Third-Party Data Is Used

The data collected by third-party providers may be used for:

  • Displaying advertisements (non-personalized or personalized based on user consent)
  • Improving app performance and stability
  • Detecting bugs and optimizing gameplay
  • Understanding gameplay trends and user engagement

4. Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for general audiences and is not designed for children under 4. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately so we can take action.

5. Consent and Control

Upon installing or launching the app, you may be asked to provide consent for personalized ads. Users may choose to:

  • Allow personalized ads
  • Limit ad tracking through device settings
  • Reset advertising identifiers

You may change these options anytime in your device's privacy settings.

6. Data Retention

We do not store any personal information on our servers. Third-party services retain anonymized data only as long as necessary for analytics, advertising, or crash diagnostics.

7. Security

We prioritize maintaining a secure environment by relying on trusted third-party providers with strong security systems. While no digital system is 100% secure, we make reasonable efforts to protect data handled by our app.

8. Virtual Currency Clarification

Stack & Match 3D: Arcade Build does not include real or virtual currency. Any points, coins, scores, or rewards shown in the game are purely fictional gameplay mechanics with no real-world value.

These points cannot be transferred, redeemed, traded, or exchanged for real currency or goods, and are used only for in-game scoring and progression.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
