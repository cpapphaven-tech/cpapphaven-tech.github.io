Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when you use our mobile application “Stack & Match 3D” (also includes Stack 3D and Cube Match 3D gameplay modes).

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email, phone number, or location.

We do not create user accounts, store passwords, or track any personally identifiable data.

2. Data Collected by Third-Party Services

Our app uses third-party services that may collect non-personal device information for analytics or advertising. These may include:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Google Firebase (analytics, crash reports)

These services may collect:

  • Device model, OS version
  • App usage data
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA)

All data collected is handled by the respective third parties under their privacy policies.

3. How We Use the Information

Information collected by third parties is used only for:

  • Improving app performance
  • Providing non-personalized or personalized ads (based on user consent)
  • Identifying and fixing crashes

4. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe data was collected accidentally, contact us and we will remove it.

5. Data Retention

We do not store any personal information. Third-party tools retain anonymized data according to their own policies.

6. Consent & Opt-Out

On first launch, users may grant or deny permission for personalized ads. You may also reset your advertising ID (IDFA) in iOS settings.
