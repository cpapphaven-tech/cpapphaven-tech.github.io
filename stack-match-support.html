Frequently Asked Questions

1. The game is lagging or running slowly

Try closing background apps or restarting your device. For best performance, ensure your device is updated to the latest iOS version.

2. Ads are not loading

  • Check your internet connection
    • li>Ensure you did not disable tracking permissions (IDFA)
  • Some regions restrict certain ad formats

3. My progress did not save

Stack & Match 3D stores high scores on your device using internal storage. If the app was reinstalled or data was cleared, progress may reset.

4. How do I disable personalized ads?

You can manage ad preferences from:

Settings → Privacy → Tracking

About the Game

Stack & Match 3D: Arcade Build offers two unique gameplay modes:

  • STACK 3D – Time your taps, stack blocks perfectly, and chase the highest score.
  • CUBE MATCH 3D – Match 3 identical objects before your tray fills up.

We hope you enjoy the experience and continue to challenge your skills!
